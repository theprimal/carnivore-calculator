Free Carnivore Guides/Resources

I found these free guides and resources very helpful in my carnivore journey. To add your resources, please contact me xshape [at] gmail [dot] com.

Free Carnivore YouTube Channels

  • The Primal Podcast (480k Subscribers)

    Focuses on discussions related to the primal and carnivore lifestyles, offering valuable interviews with experts in health and wellness.

  • Anthony Chaffee (474k Subscribers)

    Dr. Anthony Chaffee explores the science behind the carnivore diet and promotes its health benefits through expert interviews and personal experience.

  • Shawn Baker (345k Subscribers)

    A leading advocate for the carnivore diet, Dr. Shawn Baker shares insights on health, fitness, and the benefits of a meat-based diet.

  • Laura Spath (366k Subscribers)

    Laura Spath offers tips, recipes, and insights on living a healthy carnivore lifestyle, along with her personal experiences on the diet.

  • Steak and Butter Gal (342k Subscribers)

    Focuses on simplifying the carnivore diet, with personal advice, tips, and inspiration for anyone looking to adopt a carnivore diet.

  • Jenny Mitich (132k Subscribers)

    Jenny Mitich shares her journey with the carnivore diet, offering advice on health, wellness, and how a meat-only diet can improve overall well-being.

  • Ketogenic Woman (110k Subscribers)

    This channel explores the intersection of keto and carnivore diets, with great carnivore-friendly recipes.

Free Carnivore Blogs & Websites

  • The Primal Blog – The most comprehensive resource on carnivore diet, and the carnivore lifestyle. Lots of free resources and recipes.
  • Dr. Kiltz – Insights on fertility, health, and the benefits of a meat-based diet from Dr. Robert Kiltz.
  • Carnivore MD (Dr. Paul Saladino) – Science-based insights on the carnivore diet and optimal health.
  • Carnivore.diet – A resource hub with success stories, meal plans, and carnivore diet coaching.
  • Kevin Stock – A deep dive into the science and practicality of the carnivore diet.
  • Zero Carb Zen – Personal experiences and philosophical insights on the zero-carb lifestyle.
