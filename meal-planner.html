What is a Carnivore Meal Planner?

A Carnivore Meal Planner is a handy tool that helps you plan carnivore-friendly meals (only include animal-based foods). It adjust your meals with your daily calories, and making it easier to stick to a carnivore diet.

Carnivore Foods

The carnivore diet only includes animal-based foods and excludes plants. Common options are: Meats: Beef (e.g., ribeye steak, ground beef), pork (e.g., pork belly), lamb, and organ meats like liver. Poultry: Chicken thighs, duck, and other fowl. Seafood: Salmon, mackerel, sardines, oysters, shrimp, and lobster. Dairy: Butter, cheddar cheese, and other full-fat cheeses. Eggs.
